About Front-End Lead, Danny Collan

Danny's favorite hobbies are listening to music, running at the park, and going out to have a good time.

He is a computer science major attending San Francisco State University who aspires to work as a

software engineer one day for a great company, with big intentions to write his own application software

in the near future. Danny's favorite cuisines include famous Italian recipes, particularly Fettucini

Alfredo, along with several other choices of the same type. He is currently serving as the Front-End

lead for a current software project, where it becomes his responsibility to ensure the success of the

beautiful design for a web application. To describe his character, Danny is a worker who puts in all that

he can apply to making sure the task is well-completed, and ensures that no matter how challenging something

may become, he does not give up on the task at hand. He is a very courteous and polite kind of person who

understands the outcomes of certain situations, and enjoys the times he spends with certain groups of any

kind. Though he can sometimes be quiet, Danny is a faithful young adult who likes to keep up his positivity.
